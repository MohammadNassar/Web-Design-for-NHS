St Andrew's Health Centre is both a GP surgery serving over 4,000 patients and a Walk in Centre. Registered patients can book appointments by calling 020 8980 1888. The Walk in Centre service is for people who have a need for health care and who cannot, for whatever reason, access their normal health provider. Patients using the Walk in Centre are seen on a first come first served basis. The Walk in Centre service is available from 8am to 8pm Monday to Sunday. Please note that while we aim to see patients at the Walk in Centre within a hour at busy times the wait can be as long as four hours.
