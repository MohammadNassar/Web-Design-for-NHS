Registering as a patient

Registration of new patients takes place during opening hours. Please bring with you:

  • Proof of your address e.g. a recent household bill or rent book
  • If you have a medical Card, please bring this with you as this will help us obtain your previous records.

Check to see if you are eligible to register!

To check if you fall within our catchment area, please enter your post code here and press Check
Please DO NOT include spaces.
