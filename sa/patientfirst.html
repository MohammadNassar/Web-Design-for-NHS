    • Patient First is our organisation- wide project that engages patients to become more involved in their healthcare and the organisation in a variet.

    It is refreshing and rewarding to be involved in the production of a service. Take Internet Banking, booking a holiday online, or ordering a groceries delivery as examples. We feel in control, empowered - and we encourage our patients to consider healthcare in the same way.

    We are working towards creating an environment where there is space for patients to be involved in shaping the services that are on offer. This means being innovative in the way we work together, open to suggestion and creative in meeting demands.

    To achieve this transition staff and patients will work together in a new way. Patients will be encouraged and supported to share their needs, preferences and concerns, and the surgery will be flexible so that these can be incorporated into the way services are planned and delivered.

    Ways you can be actively involved:

    • Come along to our Patient Group Meetings; they are advertised throughout the surgery and everybody is welcome
    • Pass on your comments to the Patient Assistant Team at the front desk
    • Contact Emma at the surgery to discuss any ideas that you might have
    • Look out for any feedback opportunities at the surgery

    We will ensure that every conversation counts and ultimately, we will always put the patient first.
