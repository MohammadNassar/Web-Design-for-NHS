Patient Charter

Patient Charter

We believe that there are certain expectations that patients can expect from all services that we offer. Please read below to get an idea of our vision and values as an organisation.

We value making our patients feel welcome regardless of race, religion, age, gender, sexuality or disability, and we will never discriminate against patients with complex health needs. That means…
All members of the community will be treated equally at all times. Your health needs will be addressed personally and appropriately.

We aim to provide a welcoming atmosphere for patients and visitors, which means when you visit…
Members of staff will greet you in a compassionate, polite, personal and approachable way.
All comments and queries will be listened and responded to accurately.
You can be confident of being safe which is why we will challenge all aggressive behaviour.

We value partnership working with other organisations. That means you can expect…
That referrals, recommendations and suggestions made are done so in the belief that the service you will receive is of a high quality.
We will support local organisations to encourage patients and local people to lead healthy lifestyles.

We value empowering people to help themselves and we believe in the impacts of wider determinants of health. That means…
We will give helpful and relevant advice about keeping yourself and your family healthy.
That your individual circumstances will be considered, and choice will be offered accordingly.
That patients and staff will work as a team to achieve shared health goals.
We will encourage and support you to attend all of your appointments, and missed appointments will be followed up.

We aim to be open to new ideas and new ways of working, which means that...
All staff will listen to patients and plan improvements from what they have heard.
We will provide a variety of ways for patients to share their thoughts and opinions about the surgery.
We will treat complaints as a chance to learn and improve.

We will be widely known for our high patient satisfaction, and we will know this through…
Promoting the patient voice and feedback from patients and encouraging involvement in a variety of ways.
The results of inspections carried out and published by the Care Quality Commission.
Thoughts and opinions provided via Tower Hamlets Health Watch.

We value giving all members of the team the opportunity to develop to their full potential, and we aim to provide high quality training for a wide range of health professionals so you can expect…
To be informed of new services/treatments available
For our services to continue to grow and improve
To be informed when there will be medical students present, and always be able to opt out.
