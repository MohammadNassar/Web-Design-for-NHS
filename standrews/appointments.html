Book an Appointment

Book an appointment

To make appointments you can call our reception on 020 8980 1888 or visit the practice in the opening hours.

Registered patients may also use the walk in service for urgent needs at the weekends. But do note that this operates as a walk in and you are not able to book specific appointment times over the phone.

It is possible to request routine appointments with specific doctors. For the same day appointments you may have to see whatever doctor is available. If you require a home visit you will need to talk to the on call doctor who will discuss with you why the visit is necessary and when it can happen. The doctors do home visits after morning surgery. Please ring 020 8980 1888 before 11.00am if possible.

If you need urgent advice, ring the put-of-hours service on 020 7377 7151. You may wish to contact NHS Direct either by phone on 0845 4647 or online at www.nhsdirect.nhs.uk

You can also book appointments online. Please go to the Online Access tab, Book an appointment to do this.
