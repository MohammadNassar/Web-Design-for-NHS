Book an appointment

To make appointments you must call our reception on 0844 815 1020.

You can also use the walk in Centre at St. Andrew’s when we are closed at 2 Hannaford Walk, Devon’s road, E3 3FF. It is open 8am-8pm seven days a week. But do note that this operates as a walk in and you are not able to book specific appointment times over the phone.

It is possible to request routine appointments with specific doctors. For the same day appointments you may have to see whatever doctor is available. If you need a home visit you will need to talk to the on call doctor who will discuss with you why the visit is necessary and when it can happen. The doctors do home visits after morning surgery. Please ring 0844 815 1020 before 11.00am if possible.

If you need urgent advice, ring the put-of-hours service on 020 7377 7151. You may wish to contact NHS Direct either by phone on 0845 4647 or online at www.nhsdirect.nhs.uk

You can also book appointments online. You will need a special login to do this-just ask at reception.
