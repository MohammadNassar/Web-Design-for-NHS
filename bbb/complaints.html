Complaints procedure

Sometimes patients are dissatisfied with the service they receive. We are keen to hear about these experiences so we can learn from them and make improvements accordingly.

There are several ways you can contact is with regards to negative experiences.

  1. Write a formal letter of complaint, addressed to the practice Manager, via the surgery
  2. Leave a comment on NHS Choices www.nhs.uk
